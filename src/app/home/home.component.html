From the olden times man had worn something to protect himself from heat and cold of nature

The fashion in dress has changed many times. In the olden days in India man used to wear dhoti, kurta or shirt and women sarees or lehnga. Then came pant-shirt for man and salwar suits for women.

  • The colours and cuts have also changed pattern

  • Sometimes it is loose fitting and sometimes it is skin fit.
  • Sometimes it is three piece suit and the other time it is two piece suit.
  • A man who is neatly dressed and is wearing polished shoes gives the impression of his clean habit. So the dress gives some idea about the habit of the wearer.
